Dr Raphael Kamanga

Director and Principal Consultant

Leadwood Solutions

Dr. Raphael Kamanga is a specialist and strategist providing expertise in business and digital transformation. With over 30 years of experience across sectors like ICT, telecommunications, fintech, banking, and health, he has led innovative digital strategies and transformation projects for over a decade.

He holds a PhD in Information Systems from the University of South Africa and an MSc from Leeds Beckett University. His deep technical background is complemented by professional certifications in X-Road, Cisco, ITIL, and Microsoft. Having served at executive management levels for two decades, Dr. Kamanga brings a wealth of knowledge in AutoML data analytics, Open Finance, and Data Exchange solutions to Leadwood Solutions.
